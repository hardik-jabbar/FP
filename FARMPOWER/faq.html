What materials are used in your Tractors?

We use high-quality, durable materials in all our farming tractors. Our components include reinforced steel frames, fuel-efficient engines, and advanced hydraulic systems. We ensure that every tractor is built to withstand tough farming conditions while maintaining peak performance. You can find specific details on each product page.

How do I maintain my Tractor

Regular maintenance is key to extending the life of your tractor. We recommend routine oil changes, checking tire pressure, inspecting hydraulic systems, and keeping the engine clean. Detailed maintenance instructions can be found in your tractor's user manual and on our website.

Are your tractors environmentally friendly?

Yes, sustainability is at the core of our engineering. We use fuel-efficient engines to minimize emissions, source eco-friendly materials when possible, and design tractors that reduce soil impact. We are also investing in alternative energy-powered tractors for the future. Learn more on our Sustainability page. Sustainability page.

In most cases, you don’t need a license for private farm use. However, road use regulations vary by location, so check with local authorities.
