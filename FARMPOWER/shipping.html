Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about our shipping policies, delivery times, and tracking

Domestic Shipping

Shipping Options

We offer several shipping options for deliveries within the United States:

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-5 business days $6.95 Free on orders over $75
Expedited Shipping 2-3 business days $12.95
Express Shipping 1-2 business days $19.95

Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding holidays. Orders placed after 1 PM EST will be processed the following business day.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing may take up to 3 business days. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Shipping to Alaska and Hawaii

We ship to Alaska and Hawaii using USPS Priority Mail. Additional shipping charges may apply, and delivery times may be longer than those for the continental United States. Please allow 5-7 business days for delivery to these locations.

International Shipping Options

International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Available options will be calculated at checkout based on your shipping address and the items in your cart.

Generally, you can expect:

  • Standard International: 7-14 business days
  • Express International: 3-7 business days

Customs, Duties, and Taxes

International orders may be subject to import duties, taxes, and customs fees imposed by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping costs. Please check with your local customs office for more information about potential additional charges before placing your order.

Order Tracking

All orders include tracking information, which will be sent to you via email once your order ships. You can also track your order in the following ways:

1

Account Order History

Log into your account and navigate to "Order History." Select the order you wish to track to view its current status and tracking information.

2

Order Tracking Page

Visit our Track Order page and enter your order number and email address to view the current status of your order.

3

Carrier Website

Use the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email on the carrier's website (USPS, UPS, FedEx, or DHL) for detailed tracking information.

Additional Shipping Policies

Address Verification

Please ensure your shipping address is correct at checkout. We use address verification systems, but you are responsible for providing accurate shipping information. Orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by customers cannot be refunded.

Delivery Issues

If your package is marked as delivered but you haven't received it, please check with neighbors, building management, or your local post office. If you still can't locate your package, contact us within 7 days of the delivery date, and we'll help resolve the issue.

Sustainable Shipping

We're committed to reducing the environmental impact of our shipping practices. We use recycled packaging materials, optimize package sizes to reduce waste, and are working toward carbon-neutral shipping. Learn more about our sustainability efforts on our Sustainability page.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will my order ship?

Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days after you place your order. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order is on its way.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Address changes can only be accommodated if the order hasn't been processed yet. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible if you need to change your shipping address.

Do you ship to P.O. boxes?

Yes, we do ship to P.O. boxes for standard shipping orders. However, expedited and express shipping options may not be available for P.O. box addresses. Some larger items may also require a physical street address for delivery.
