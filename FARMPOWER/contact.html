Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tractors, equipment, and smart farming services

What financing options are available for tractors?

We offer flexible financing options including leasing, rent-to-own, and traditional financing with competitive interest rates. Our financial experts can help you choose the best option for your farming needs and budget.

How does the GPS tracking and mapping service work?

Our GPS tracking system provides real-time location data, equipment usage monitoring, and detailed field mapping. The service includes a user-friendly dashboard where you can track your fleet, analyze field data, and generate reports for optimized farming operations.

What maintenance services do you provide?

We offer comprehensive maintenance services including scheduled maintenance, emergency repairs, remote diagnostics, and preventive maintenance programs. Our certified technicians are trained to work on all major tractor brands and farming equipment.

Can I trade in my old tractor?

Yes, we accept trade-ins for both new and used equipment purchases. Our experts will evaluate your equipment and provide a fair market value. We also offer a buy-back program for equipment purchased from us.

What training and support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive training for all new equipment purchases, including operation, maintenance, and smart features. Our 24/7 support team is available for technical assistance, and we offer regular workshops on advanced farming techniques.
