Returns & Exchanges

Our hassle-free policy ensures your complete satisfaction with every purchase

Return Policy Overview

At Essentials, we stand behind the quality of our products and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not entirely happy with your order, we're here to help.

Quick Summary

  • 30-day return period from the date of delivery
  • Items must be unused with original tags and packaging
  • Free return shipping for customers in the United States
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your return

Return Process

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

1

Initiate Your Return

Log into your account and go to "Order History." Select the order and items you wish to return and follow the prompts. If you checked out as a guest, use our Guest Return Portal with your order number and email address.

2

Print Your Return Label

Once your return is approved, you'll receive a prepaid return shipping label via email. Print this label and attach it to your package. If you don't have access to a printer, contact our customer service team for alternative options.

3

Pack Your Items

Carefully pack the items you're returning in their original packaging or a secure alternative. Include all original tags, accessories, and any paperwork that came with the items. Please don't use markings or labels on the original product packaging.

4

Ship Your Return

Drop off your package at any authorized carrier location (USPS, UPS, or FedEx, depending on your return label). We recommend getting a tracking receipt for your records. Returns typically take 5-10 business days to reach our warehouse.

5

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund has been processed. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product, we recommend placing a new order for the desired item and returning the original purchase using our standard return process. This ensures you receive your preferred item as quickly as possible.

For special circumstances or assistance with exchanges, please contact our customer service team. We're happy to help coordinate an exchange for you.

Return Eligibility

Eligible for Return

Items that meet the following criteria are eligible for return:

  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Unused, unworn, and unwashed
  • In original condition with all tags attached
  • In original or equivalent packaging
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

Not Eligible for Return

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items marked as final sale or clearance
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Gift cards
  • Items that have been used, washed, or worn (except for defective items)
  • Items without the original tags and packaging
  • Items returned after 30 days from delivery

Defective Items & Exceptions

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order. Provide photos of the defect or damage to help us assess the issue. We'll work with you to resolve the problem promptly, either by replacing the item or issuing a refund.

In the rare case that you receive an incorrect item, please contact us immediately. We'll arrange for the return of the incorrect item and ensure you receive the item you originally ordered.

Special Circumstances

We understand that special circumstances may arise. If you have a unique situation regarding a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team. We're committed to customer satisfaction and will work with you to find a solution.

International Returns

We accept returns from international customers within 30 days of delivery. However, return shipping costs for international orders are the responsibility of the customer, and we are unable to provide prepaid return labels for international shipments.

To initiate an international return, please contact our customer service team for instructions. We recommend using a tracked shipping method for your return. Please note that international returns may take longer to process, and refund times may vary depending on your financial institution.

Any customs fees, taxes, or duties incurred during the return shipping process are the responsibility of the customer and are non-refundable.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Once processed, it may take an additional 3-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment method and financial institution.

Can I return a gift?

Yes, gifts can be returned within 30 days of the delivery date. You'll need the order number, which can be found on the gift receipt. Returns of gifts will be issued as store credit to the gift recipient, not to the original purchaser.

What if I don't have the original packaging?

We strongly recommend returning items in their original packaging to ensure they're not damaged during transit. If you no longer have the original packaging, please use a sturdy box or mailer and ensure the item is well-protected. Items damaged during return shipping due to inadequate packaging may not be eligible for a full refund.
