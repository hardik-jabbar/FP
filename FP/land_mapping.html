Land Usage Plans for Your Fields

North Paddock - Land Plan

Suggested Crops:

  • Tomatoes
  • Wheat
  • Soybeans

Season Plan:

Kharif Season (June - Sep): Tomatoes are recommended due to good market demand and suitable soil conditions. Ensure proper staking and pest control.
Rabi Season (Oct - Mar): Wheat is a good follow-up crop. Use certified seeds for better yield.
Consider crop rotation with Soybeans next year (after Wheat harvest) to improve soil nitrogen content and overall health.

River Bend Field - Land Plan

Suggested Crops:

  • Sugarcane
  • Mustard

Season Plan:

Annual Crop (Feb planting): Sugarcane is suitable for this field given its proximity to water and soil type. This will occupy the field for the majority of the year. Focus on high-yielding varieties.
Intercropping (Optional): Consider short-duration legumes during early stages of sugarcane growth.
After sugarcane harvest (approx. 10-12 months), plant Mustard as a rabi crop to utilize residual moisture and nutrients. This also helps in pest cycle breaking.
